# Human-led AI agent teams with Squad 🥊

**Event:** build:// localhost · Oslo 2026
**Speaker:** Haflidi — Microsoft
**Connect:** [LinkedIn · in/haflidif](https://www.linkedin.com/in/haflidif/)

A demo-heavy session on **Squad** — giving any repo its own team of AI
specialists that live in the repo as files, persist across sessions, share
decisions, and turn your **GitHub Issues into pull requests** — while you stay
the human who says *merge*.

## In this folder

- 📊 **[Squad-Oslo-2026-Slides.pdf](./Squad-Oslo-2026-Slides.pdf)** — the session
  slides (with clickable links).
- 🛠️ **[TRY-IT-YOURSELF.md](./TRY-IT-YOURSELF.md)** — a hands-on, step-by-step
  guide so you can reproduce the whole demo yourself, from `squad init` to
  Issues → PRs.
